ABOUT US

At Blueprints, we provide high-quality construction clothing designed to keep you safe and comfortable on the job. From durable hardhats to reflective vests, our gear is built to meet the demands of hard-working professionals. We've got you covered from the ground up!

MEET THE TEAM

Head Designer Jim Morgan

Jim Morgan has over 15 years of experience designing construction apparel, specializing in functional and durable gear for workers. He leads Blueprints' design team, focusing on blending innovation with practicality to create clothing that stands up to the toughest work environments. Jim is committed to ensuring each product meets the highest standards of safety and comfort for construction professionals.

Owner Sam Bernard

As the founder and owner of Blueprints, Sam Bernard has built the company from the ground up with a dedication to providing high-quality construction clothing. His hands-on approach and deep understanding of the industry's needs drive the company's success. Sam ensures that Blueprints remains focused on delivering dependable, affordable gear to hardworking construction professionals.

CEO Kim Sanford

Kim Sanford serves as the CEO of Blueprints, leading the company with a strong focus on growth and innovation. With a background in leadership and business strategy, Kim is committed to expanding Blueprints' reach while maintaining its reputation for quality. Kim works closely with the team to ensure that every product meets the needs of construction workers and exceeds expectations.
